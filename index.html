Research

In the past I've worked in software engineering, HRI, and most recently human-in-the-loop reinforcement learning. I am now more interested in patterns/representations/behaviors that emerge without the need for human demonstrations/instruction/labels. To that end, I'd love to work with self-supervised, unsupervised, or reinforcement learning (no-human-in-the-loop) in the future; however, I have still only explored a small amount of possible fields and am open to other areas of research as well.
